A padlock anyone can snap shut (public key), but only you have the key to open (private key).

‚ö° Too slow for big data. Use it to securely exchange an AES key, then encrypt data with AES (hybrid encryption).

‚ö° For push-only notifications, use FCM (Android) or APNs (iOS) instead. MQTT shines when devices also send data back.
